Seven centuries ago, the Age of Pirates came to an end. Since then, humanity has been under the rule of the Celestials. Clipse Lionheart has only ever known captivity. That is, until her mother’s plan — a plan that could change everything — leads Clipse and a crew of escaped humans on a journey across the horizon. Clipse learns of the world beyond the city of Zxu; a world with secrets and powers fighting for control. Little does she know, a deadly balance has been established, and one misstep could ruin everything.



About the Book

Pirates is the first book that I have ever written, and subsequently published. It was published in June of 2023, and took me roughly two years to write it. All of the artwork you will see below was drawn by me, and all graphics relating to the book were also self-made, such as the cover, map, and character art. The story follows Clipse Lionheart, a young pirate girl whose personal quest for freedom develops into something much bigger.
